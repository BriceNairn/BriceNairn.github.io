<h3><a href="https://github.com/BriceNairn/amibetteroff-notes">Read my developer notes</a></h3>

Building software is about more than writing code. Every project involves decisions around product design, architecture, infrastructure, deployment, analytics and growth.

This developer journal documents the journey of building and operating AmIBetterOff.au, a salary comparison and employment analysis tool designed to help Australians better understand the financial impact of career decisions. The articles cover the challenges, trade-offs and lessons learned while taking the project from idea to production.

Since launch, the platform has attracted thousands of visitors through organic search, achieved first-page Google rankings for multiple salary comparison searches, and provided valuable insight into product development, user behaviour and technical operations.

<h3>Topics covered include:</h3>

- Product design and feature prioritisation
- User experience and interface design
- Application architecture and infrastructure
- Cloudflare deployment and edge hosting
- Search engine optimisation and discoverability
- Analytics and performance monitoring
- Compliance and legal considerations
- Launch strategy and user feedback
- Future enhancements and roadmap planning

The goal of these notes is not simply to document what was built, but to capture the reasoning behind key technical and product decisions. They provide a behind-the-scenes look at the realities of designing, building, launching and maintaining a real-world web application as an independent developer.
